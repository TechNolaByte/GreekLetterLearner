Notes about Phonetic Source:

		
It is extremely difficult to discern which pronunciations are "correct".

		
I have tried matching our Machen textbook as closely as possible, since that is what we are using.

		
But have made slight modification according to 
https://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Koine_Greek/1._Alphabet,_Pronunciation,_and_Punctuation

		
Really the only important part for our studies is that we all agree and that different letters have as little ambiguity as possible when spoken.
